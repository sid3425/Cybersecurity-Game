Rules of H@CK4R Z0NE

The objective of the game is to defeat the hacker by reducing their health to 0 or below. You do this by selecting the correct card to counter the hacker's attack.

Gameplay

Each round, the hacker will present an attack card with a power level. You have three defense cards to choose from, each with its own power level.

Select the defense card you believe is the strongest counter to the hacker's attack.

  • If your chosen card's power level is HIGHER than the hacker's, you deal damage equal to the difference to the hacker. You successfully defended the attack!
  • If your chosen card's power level is LOWER than the hacker's, the hacker deals damage equal to the difference to you. You were hacked!
  • If the power levels are equal, it's a tie, and no damage is dealt.

Winning and Losing

The game continues until either your health or the hacker's health reaches 0 or below.

  • If the hacker's health reaches 0 or below first, you win!
  • If your health reaches 0 or below first, you lose.
